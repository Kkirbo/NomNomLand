Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At La Funzione, we are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, make a reservation, or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Name and contact details (email address, phone number)
  • Reservation details
  • Delivery address information
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party providers)
  • Technical data such as IP address and browser type

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process reservations and orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send newsletters (only if you have subscribed)

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Your consent
  • The performance of a contract (e.g., reservations or orders)
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Our legitimate business interests

4. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or to comply with legal requirements.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared with trusted service providers (such as payment processors or hosting providers) strictly for operational purposes.

6. Your Rights

In accordance with applicable data protection laws (including GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at: contact@lafunzione.food
